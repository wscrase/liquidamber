Professional Tree Surgery Services

Expert care for your trees, from felling to maintenance. Keeping your landscape safe and healthy.

Tree Removal & Felling

Whether you need to remove a tree due to disease, storm damage, or safety concerns, our professional tree felling services ensure safe and efficient removal. We specialise in controlled dismantling, particularly for trees in tight spaces or near buildings, using advanced rigging techniques to prevent damage.

Our expert arborists assess each tree carefully, ensuring compliance with local tree preservation regulations before removal. If you're unsure whether a tree needs felling or pruning, we provide consultations to help you make the right decision.

Crown Reductions & Thinning

A crown reduction is a precise pruning technique used to reduce the overall size of a tree while maintaining its natural shape. By carefully shortening each branch, we can improve light penetration, reduce wind resistance, and enhance the tree’s health and longevity.

This service is ideal for trees that have become too large for their surroundings or pose a risk due to excessive weight. Our team ensures all cuts are made to appropriate growth points, promoting healthy regrowth while preserving the tree's structure.

Deadwood Removal

Over time, trees naturally develop dead branches, which can pose a significant risk if left unchecked. Our deadwood removal service eliminates hazardous branches, reducing the risk of falling limbs that could endanger property, pedestrians, or vehicles.

Regular deadwood removal is essential for maintaining tree health and ensuring safety in residential gardens, public parks, and commercial landscapes. Our expert arborists inspect each tree thoroughly, identifying and removing any unstable limbs.

Hedge Cutting & Maintenance

Our hedge cutting service ensures your hedges remain neat, healthy, and well-shaped throughout the year. Whether you need a simple trim or a complete hedge reduction, we have the expertise to manage all types of hedging, including large conifer screens, formal box hedges, and mixed native hedgerows.

Regular hedge maintenance improves curb appeal, enhances privacy, and promotes healthy regrowth. We offer both one-off hedge cutting services and scheduled maintenance plans to keep your outdoor space looking its best.

Stump Grinding & Removal

Tree stumps can be unsightly and take up valuable space in your garden or commercial property. Our stump grinding service removes stumps efficiently, allowing for replanting, landscaping, or construction in the cleared area.

We use professional stump grinders to safely remove stumps and surface roots, preventing regrowth and potential trip hazards. Whether you need a single stump removed or multiple stumps cleared from a site, we provide a fast and efficient solution.
