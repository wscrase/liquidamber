Tree Surgery

Expert tree pruning, felling, and maintenance services for your trees, ensuring their health, safety, and longevity. Our tree surgeons are certified and use modern techniques for the best results.

  • Tree Pruning & Shaping: Enhance the health and appearance of your trees.
  • Tree Felling: Safe and controlled removal of dangerous or unwanted trees.
  • Tree Stump Grinding: Efficient removal of stumps to improve your landscape.
  • Emergency Tree Services: Rapid response for storm damage or hazardous trees.
  • Tree Health Assessments: Professional evaluations to ensure long-term tree vitality.

Tree Consultancy

Providing expert advice on tree management, preservation, and legal considerations to help you make informed decisions about your trees and landscape.

  • Tree Preservation Orders (TPOs): Guidance on the legal protections for trees in your area.
  • Tree Risk Assessment: Identifying potential hazards to prevent future damage or injury.
  • Landscape Design & Tree Integration: Advice on integrating trees into your property’s design.
  • Planning Permission Support: Assisting with applications involving trees and development projects.
  • Tree Management Plans: Long-term strategies for maintaining the health and safety of your trees.
